OilWellStore.com is THE place for online oil well investing.

OilWellStore.com offers non-operated working interest limited partnerships. Each interactive partnership presentation provides direct and transparent access to information about the Managing Partner, Operator and Partnership being offered. Whether you have invested before or are a new partner, it is important for you to know the duties and responsibilities of the Managing Partner and Operator.

The interactive Partnership Presentation Pallet (The Pallet) is designed to address the most common questions and concerns when it comes to educating, evaluating, and purchasing an oil drilling partnership. As you will soon find, The Pallet will generate a unique investment scenario for you to help assist in your decision to invest. The interactive presentation is displayed in 4 sections:

  1. Close-ology Map
  2. Hypothetical Price, Production, and Cash Flow Scenarios
  3. Tax Effect
  4. Partnership Presentation Results

SECTION 1 - Interactive Close-ology Map

This Interactive Close-ology Map is used for you to research surrounding wells within the proposed drilling area. Clicking on a plotted well location generates specific information such as: API#, Operator, Well Status, Dates of Production, Barrels of Oil Produced, Average Oil Price and Estimated Gross Cash Flow. The window to the right of the map takes this data, and compares it with the same levels of oil production and gross cash flow across the terms of the partnership being presented. In the same window, Your Estimated Oil Price can be changed to any value so that you can explore different potential pricing scenarios within the terms of the intended partnership. 

You might be asking yourself, Why do I need to know about surrounding well production in the area? Here is why-exploring oil production from wells in the immediate area, under the terms of the partnership can help determine a reasonable expectation of the proposed well.

The Interactive Close-ology Map is powered by DrillingInfo.com data.  OilWellStore.com and DrillingInfo.com have established a business relationship which provides prospects with direct access to the most comprehensive oil and gas well information available.

SECTION 3 - Tax Effect
 

The Tax Effect Tool helps partners and prospects estimate the impact of an oil drilling partnership on their Estimated After Tax Income. By inputting Your Estimated Taxable, and choosing Your Tax Filing Status the tool will display a side-by-side comparison of the impact with or without a K-1 SEC Regulation D Rule 506(c) partnerships provide investing partners with K-1™s for your tax filing purposes. So what is a K-1 and why is it important? A Schedule K-1, or K-1, is a tax document that reports all incomes, deductions, credits and losses associated with your share of any partnership in which you are participating.

Utilizing the tool can help you decide your optimal Investment Amount for each filing situation as well as estimate the number of Actual Dollars at Risk. IMPORTANT: This is not tax advice, but merely an interactive tool showing you the potential tax benefits associated with investing in an oil drilling partnership.
